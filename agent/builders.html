Builders

Win the first month.

The first month of an agent's career is the most important. Follow the targets below alongside daily feedback from your manager.

Set the standards

01

Consistency

Show up the same way every day. Habits build the production.

02

Standards

Hold the bar for yourself, your prospects, and your team.

03

Confidence

Belief sells. Lead the conversation and trust the process you've been taught.

Discovery questions

Ask each new agent in their first conversation. Their answers shape every coaching session that follows.

"

What are your goals for yourself?

Listen for specifics like income, lifestyle, or time freedom. Generic answers mean you dig deeper.

"

What are you hoping to get out of this?

Their why is what you'll come back to on tough days. Write it down.

Setting expectations

  • Consistent schedule. Same start time, same end time, every day.
  • On Zoom, unmuted, camera on. Trainings only work when you're in them. Show up ready to roleplay.
  • Walk the script in detail. Walk through every page and every line. Know why each word is there before you touch a phone.

Onboarding checklist

From exam day to your first dial. Work through each phase in order.

1
Pre-exam

Lock in your exam.

  • Schedule your exam. Two-week deadline. Send proof — screenshot or text confirmation — to admin.
  • Claim your free course voucher.
  • Pass the exam.
2
Post-exam

Apply for your license.

  • Create a dedicated work Gmail. Format: firstnamelastname@gmail.com — text the new address to admin.
  • Apply for your state license.
  • Schedule fingerprints. If your state requires it — schedule after applying for your license.
3
While you wait

Build the foundation.

  • Join the team Discord and GroupMe. Links live on Quick Links.
  • Complete boot camp.
  • Attend Sales Mastery and Closerz Academy trainings.
  • Study the sales script and rebuttals.
4
Post-license approval

Launch and dial.

  • Check for your National Producer Number (NPN). Usually available about a week after license approval.
  • Purchase E&O insurance. Select the lowest option (Option A). Send proof to admin.
  • Submit a voided check. For commission deposit setup. Send to admin.
  • Complete contracting with carriers. Schedule a Zoom appointment with admin to walk through it.
  • Start dialing and training. Use paper applications until your carrier approvals come through.

Once you're past the checklist, your day-to-day cadence lives on the Training page. Each week unlocks once you finish the previous one.
