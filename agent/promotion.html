Starting tier

Qualification rules

To qualify for a new contract level: hit the required production for two consecutive months. Re-qualify one month out of every four following your promotion or you'll move down one level.

Producer contract guidelines

First two months

Lead assistance & training

Focus on lead assistance and training. These months don't count toward production milestones.

90-day rule

$15K minimum by month 3

All team members must issue at least $15,000 in monthly revenue by the end of their third month. Failure to meet this standard results in dismissal.

Month 3 onward

Production tracking begins

Once you've begun purchasing leads, your production is officially tracked and eligible for promotion.

Builder contract guidelines

  • $80K max monthly personal production qualifies toward a builder contract.
  • To qualify for the 120% builder tier, you must take over contracting admin tasks.
